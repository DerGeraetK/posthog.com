### Q3 2026 objectives

<details>
<summary>Make every ad ladder up to self-driving (Brian)</summary>

- **Rationale:** Every product we used to run ads for is now a tool that ladders up into self-driving, so every dollar we spend should be doing two jobs at once, building awareness of self-driving and increasing eagerness to try and adopt self-driving and PostHog Code.
- **Things we could do:** Work self-driving, engineering loops, and PostHog Code and the Slack app into our search copy, landing pages, and videos, so the whole funnel shares how each PostHog tool is great, but even better when used via self-driving.
- **We'll know we're successful when:** You can look at any ad, any landing page, or any piece of content we're running and see that it's preaching the gospel of self-driving.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Democratize ads and marketing data (Brian)</summary>

- **Rationale:** Right now if a team wants to know how their marketing is doing they have to ask me or wait for the growth review, and that's just too slow. Everyone should be able to see how their efforts are performing in real time, measured the same way, from a single source of truth, so they can adjust as they go instead of waiting on me.
- **Things we could do:** Build that single source of truth (a skill) so the Slack app can answer questions about it in plain language, and feed the same data into a dashboard that does most of the work of building the monthly growth review for me.
- **We'll know we're successful when:** Anyone can pull their results straight from the Slack app, and the monthly growth review is a two hour job instead of a two day job.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Expand platforms significantly. (Brian + Jonah)</summary>

- **Rationale:** In pushing TOFU Self-Driving, increasing our reach is necessary. By expanding to new platforms, we can reach our ICP in more ways.
- **Things we could do:** Place CTV, Meta, Tiktok, Product Hunt, Blind
- **We'll know we have success when:** Our diversified reach increases ad frequency and awareness, bringing in more customers into the PostHog universe.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Maximalist variation. Ensure we have many versions of each ad for algos to pick which is best. (Jonah)</summary>

- **Rationale:** Hooks make or break the success of videos. Making a conscious effort to test and optimize here increases the longevity of a video asset.
- **Things we could do:** Test music, UGC-style creator intros, hog animations, 3D text effect in situ.
- **We'll know we have success when:** We can look across different hooks for 1 ad asset and chart future assets based on the success of a previous ad hook.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Decrease dependency on team graphics by using Figma Weave. (Jonah)</summary>

- **Rationale:** Paid social is a numbers game. We have potent creative assets, and being able to serve them to our audience in multiple, new ways gives us more signals on effective messaging.
- **Things we could do:** Continue designing with AI tools that pull from codified brand standards. Move into animated graphics with "Hoglitos."
- **We'll know we have success when:** Graphics is not a bottleneck to ad generation. *NOTE: This not arguing to completely circumvent designers. Instead, it allows them to focus on high-impact, custom ideas while spending less time resizing, building headline-driven content, etc.*

</details>
